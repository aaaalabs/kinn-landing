### Primary Typeface Direction
**Style:** BOLD Geometric Sans-Serif - "TYROL" Energy

**Core References:**
- **TYROL poster** - Extreme boldness, angular power, geometric strength
- **SILVAPLANA poster** - Art Deco elegance but still bold and confident

### KINN Wordmark Specifications

```
Ultra-bold geometric sans-serif display font for "KINN",
inspired by 1930s Austrian Alpine tourism poster lettering,
specifically: TYROL and SILVAPLANA poster typography,

CHARACTER DETAILS:

K: 
- Extremely thick vertical stem (stroke weight 1:3 ratio to letter height)
- Strong angular diagonal extending beyond the vertical
- Sharp 45-degree angles where diagonal meets stem
- Diagonal arm has slight upward momentum

I:
- Thick vertical rectangle, no serifs
- OR subtle geometric top/bottom caps (Art Deco detail)
- Same weight as K stem for consistency

N (first):
- Heavy vertical stems
- Diagonal connector bar at 45-degree angle
- Diagonal is slightly thinner than verticals (creates visual rhythm)
- Sharp, clean angle junctions

N (second):
- Mirror of first N
- OR diagonal extends slightly to connect with first N as design flourish
- Creates visual "NN" unit that suggests "Neural Network"

OVERALL CHARACTERISTICS:
- Stroke weight: BOLD - approximately 25-30% of letter height
- Letter spacing: Tight but not touching (90-95% normal tracking)
- Baseline: Solid, confident, stable
- Height: Tall and proud, slight condensation (95% width)
- Angles: Sharp, geometric, Art Deco precision
- Feel: CONFIDENT, STRONG, ALPINE MODERNISM
- No rounded corners - keep angles sharp and geometric
- Minimal stroke contrast - monoline or near-monoline weight
```
